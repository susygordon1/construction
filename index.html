ABOUT THE COMPANY

Three generations on the same job sites.

Bedrock & Beam started as a two-person framing crew in 1998 and grew by doing the unglamorous things well: showing up on time, pricing jobs honestly, and answering the phone when something goes wrong.

Today we run four crews across residential, commercial, and renovation work, but the standard hasn't moved — every project gets a dedicated site lead, a written schedule, and a walkthrough before we call it done.
